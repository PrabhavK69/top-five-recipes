Churros

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 1/2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup sugar (for coating)
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon (for coating)

Utensils Needed

  • Medium saucepan
  • Wooden spoon
  • Mixing bowl
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Piping bag with star tip
  • Deep frying pan or pot
  • Slotted spoon
  • Paper towels

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, combine water, sugar, salt, and oil. Bring to a boil, then remove from heat.
  2. Stir in flour until mixture forms a ball.
  3. Transfer dough to a piping bag fitted with a star tip.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Pipe strips of dough into hot oil. Fry until golden brown, turning as needed.
  6. Remove churros with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
  7. Mix sugar and cinnamon in a bowl. Roll warm churros in the mixture to coat.
  8. Serve immediately, with rich chocolate sauce.
