Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients

800g grams of Chicken breast
1/2 kg tomato
1/2 kg onion
2-3 garlic cloves
3 green chilis
ginger
Chicken Tikka Masala (any brand)
1/2 kg Yoghurt

Instructions

  1. Add the yoghurt to a bowl, add salt as per taste, 1/2 teaspoon turmeric, red chili powder as per taste and one tbsp chicken tikka masala. Mix well
  2. Cut chickenbreast into small pieces. Marinate in yoghurt.
  3. While chicken is marinating, prepare the gravy. Blend cut tomatoes, onion, chili and garlic.
  4. Add 2 tbsp of oil in a pan, add cumin and bay leaves to the hot oil.
  5. Add blended gravy to the pan. Saut√© well.
  6. Add salt and pepper as per taste, 1/2 tsp of cumin and coriander powder. To this, add one tbsp Chicken Tikka Masala.
  7. Cook well, until the oil seperates out of from the gravy.
  8. Add marinated Chicken to oven at 200 degrees. bake the chicken for 20-25 minutes.
  9. Add the baked chicken to the gravy. garnish with coriander and ginger
  10. Serve hot with rice or naan
